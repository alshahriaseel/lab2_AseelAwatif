The Brown Bear

Wiki page

The brown bear (Ursus arctos) is a native to parts of northern Eurasia and northern America. Its conservation status is currently "Least Concern." There are many subspecies within the brown bear species, including the Atlas bear and Himalayn brown bear.

While the brown bear's range has shrunk, and it has faced local extinctions across its wide range, it remains listed as a least concern species by the International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN) with a total estimated population in 2017 of 110,000. .
